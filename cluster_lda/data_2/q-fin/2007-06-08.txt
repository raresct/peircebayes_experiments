0706.1247	q-fin.ST physics.data-an physics.soc-ph	Are all highly liquid securities within the same class ?	In this manuscript we analyse the leading statistical properties of fluctuations of ( log ) 3-month US Treasury bill quotation in the secondary market , namely : probability density function , autocorrelation , absolute values autocorrelation , and absolute values persistency . We verify that this financial instrument , in spite of its high liquidity , shows very peculiar properties . Particularly , we verify that log-fluctuations belong to the Levy class of stochastic variables .
