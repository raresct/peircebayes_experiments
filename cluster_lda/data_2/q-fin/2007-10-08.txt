0710.1439	q-fin.TR physics.data-an physics.soc-ph	Trading activity as driven Poisson process : comparison with empirical data	We propose the point process model as the Poissonian-like stochastic sequence with slowly diffusing mean rate and adjust the parameters of the model to the empirical data of trading activity for 26 stocks traded on NYSE . The proposed scaled stochastic differential equation provides the universal description of the trading activities with the same parameters applicable for all stocks .
