0705.2820	q-fin.TR physics.pop-ph physics.soc-ph	Entropy Oriented Trading : A Trading Strategy Based on the Second Law of Thermodynamics	The author proposes a finance trading strategy named Entropy Oriented Trading and apply thermodynamics on the strategy . The state variables are chosen so that the strategy satisfies the second law of thermodynamics . Using the law , the author proves that the rate of investment ( ROI ) of the strategy is equal to or more than the rate of price change .
